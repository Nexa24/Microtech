Advanced Fee Collections Setup

Initialize Firebase collections for advanced fee management system

What This Does

This tool initializes 9 Firebase collections required for the Advanced Fee Management System. It will create default late fee rules for each division (GAMA, LBS, CAPT) and 10 pre-built fee category templates (Admission, Exam, Lab, Library, Transport, etc.).

Collections Overview

  • installmentPlans Payment schedules and installment tracking
  • discounts Student discount records
  • scholarships Scholarship programs
  • lateFees Late fee calculations and records
  • lateFeeRules Division-specific late fee rules
  • advancePayments Advance payment tracking
  • feeBreakdowns Course fee breakdown templates
  • studentFeeBreakdowns Applied fee breakdowns per student
  • feeCategories Custom fee category definitions
